About Moses

I’m a U.S. Army veteran, attorney, and systems builder with roots in Cameroon and a life built across continents. My work lives at the intersection of law, federal systems, human psychology, and personal sovereignty.

I’m interested in how people free themselves from inherited scripts—family, culture, institutions—and design lives that reflect their actual values. That applies as much to a federal lease strategy as it does to an individuation process.

Core lenses I bring:

Strategic analysis
Risk & compliance thinking
Jungian / depth psych perspective
Masculinity & sovereignty work
Builder mindset (literal & metaphorical)

If you want consensus theatre, I’m not your guy. If you want structural honesty and a path forward, that’s where I’m useful.
